Shipping and returns

Standard shipping
If you place your order by 12pm ET, in-stock items will ship the very next business day (Monday through Friday, excluding holidays).

  • 3-5 business days.
  • Orders up to $25 have a shipping cost of $5.95
  • Orders from $25.01 to $34.99 have a shipping cost of $7.95.
  • Orders of $35 or more ship free..

Order cancellations
You’ll be able to cancel your order until it reaches the ‘In Warehouse’ status. To find out where your order is on its journey, check your account page.

Returns
All unopen returns or exchanges must be made within 90 days of receipt of your order.
